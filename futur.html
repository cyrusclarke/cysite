What will happen

Le futur nous donnera la réponse (The future will give us the answer)


What will happen in the future? Well, it will be something to look forward to, we hope. But why simply hope? Why not dream, and use dreams to take an active role in shaping the future we want! I have it on good authority that we are likely to need more than hope for a desirable future.

So here‘s a plan for the future. We want to turn a dormant space into a vibrant, thriving place. An educational institute of the future in Bangalore. A multi-disciplinary environment uniting people with different backgrounds to develop skills, think critically, act responsibly and prepare for the challenges of tomorrow.

No Grades. No Standards. No Boundaries.

We are above all looking to collaborate with like-minded people so if this sounds like something you‘d like to be involved in, read on, drop us a line, tell your friends but mostly get involved!

Cheers
